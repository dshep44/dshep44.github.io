We are a team of passionate computer technicians
The PC Guys was established in 2005 to supply computer services and sales to the Cairns northern beaches residents and businessess.

In 2014 Richard and Leanne Robertson purchased The PC Guys and with a growing team have expanded our service area to include Central Cairns and surrounding suburbs.

Our Technicians have over 30 years' experience in IT specializingin Linux, Microsft and Apple computers, notebooks, tablets and phones.

Drawing on this experience we are able to repair, build and maintain Computer hardware and software. We can set up and maintain backups, servers and networks.

Our friendly staff are here to provide a prefessional service and supply a range of products to our customers, we are always willing to advise and help with all computer issues.
